About Us


This blog was founded by Bibi Kawthar Peerbocus, Chetanand Goobodun and Idriss Koodoruth, all three of them students at the University of Mauritius that are passionate about badminton.

Indeed, Badminton sharpens timeliness and precision without overexerting bodies. This makes it a fun sport for a wide audience from the young to the old.

This blog is a space of awareness that sensitizes the audience about badminton, and allows people to communicate and share experiences.

Find us on Facebook and connect with our community. Find us on Instagram and Facebook to get in touch with our network, receive the latest news and find insightful posts. Find us on YouTube to access intuitive videos that will help your interest in badminton grow.
